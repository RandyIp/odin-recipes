Fish and Chips

Description

A Gordon Ramsay classic, fish and chips! Source

Ingredients

  • Fish (Cod Prefferably) and Potatoes (duh)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Beer

Steps

Fish

  1. Make the batter the night before, flour eggs and beer
  2. Drench fish in flour and batter
  3. Fry them fishies

Chips

  1. Cut into french fry shape
  2. Fry them taters
