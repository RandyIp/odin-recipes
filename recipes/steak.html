Steak

Description

Steak recipe from Gordon Ramsay. Works on most cuts of steak! Source

Ingredients

  • Steak (duh)
  • Salt (Preferably kosher salt)
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Make sure to take the steak out of the fridge 20 mins beforehand!
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper
  3. Make sure the pan is piping hot before putting the steak in
  4. Add oil to the pan and put the steak in by laying the steak away from you
  5. Sear both sides, roughly 30 seconds per side. (May vary depending on the size of your steak)
  6. Quickly sear the sides of the steak including the fat
  7. Crush 2 cloves of garlic and add to the pan. (Peeling is not required)
  8. Add thyme and another touch of olive oil
  9. Make sure to turn the steaks over every now and then to keep it from burning
  10. Add butter to the pan, then baste the steaks with the melted butter
  11. Finally, take the steaks out and let them rest for about 10 minutes. Then cut the steak diagonally and serve!
